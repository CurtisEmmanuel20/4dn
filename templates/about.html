About 4th Down & Numbers

4th Down & Numbers (4DN) is a cutting-edge fantasy football and sports analytics platform designed for serious players who want an edge. We go beyond surface-level stats, delivering context-aware insights powered by a blend of proprietary algorithms, expert modeling, and real-time data feeds. Whether you're building a DFS lineup, making a trade, or placing a bet, 4DN gives you the tools to make smarter, faster, and more confident decisions.

What Makes 4DN Different?

Our platform isn’t just a data dump — it’s a decision engine. We incorporate:

  • Player Readiness Scores: Factoring injury trends, matchup difficulty, team tendencies, red zone usage, and historical performance against similar defenses.
  • Elite Matchup Grading: Evaluating positional strength vs defensive schemes (e.g., WR1 vs CB1, TE vs zone coverage, slot vs man).
  • Real-Time Adjustments: Accounting for weather volatility, last-minute inactives, snap counts, and offensive line shifts.
  • Custom Efficiency Metrics: Including Yards After Contact, Explosive Play Rate, Air Yards Conversion %, and more.
  • Defensive Health Scores: A metric that captures how injured or compromised a defense is — even when big names aren’t missing.

Core Features

  • Live, AI-backed player rankings and weekly projections
  • DFS lineup optimizers with contrarian pick support
  • Smart betting edges based on over/under trends, prop value, and implied totals
  • Injury, weather, and depth chart analytics updated in real-time
  • Aggregated league news with auto-summarized takeaways
  • AI-powered matchup simulators and sit/start tools
